Tests handling of large data sets on a (simulated) slow network, with results being returned after a delay and out of order, and the total number of rows being returned at a different time to the result set.

This page uses the tests/NodeChecker plugin to make sure that the grid's nodes are correct. Rapidly drag the grid's scrollbar up and down to simulate the most pathological situations; NodeChecker will report any errors it finds to the console. Due to the fairly random nature of the testing, you may need to do this for up to 30 seconds to be sure there are no issues (although most major problems are detected pretty quickly).
